About Me

My name is Daniel Schmidt Pinheiro and over the last few years I've been working with the Microsoft technology stack. Some of the skills I'm familiar with include: C#, ASP.NET MVC, ASP.NET Web Api, Entity Framework, SQL, JavaScript, AWS, and LINQ. In 2015, I became a MCSD (Microsoft Certified Solution Developer) in Web Applications (this certification was renewed in 2017).

Also in the first semester of 2017, I was about to graduate from college and didn't really see myself going into academics (at least for now), but I still wanted to keep growing professionally. Right around that time, I saw a webinar whose title was (ironically) "How to Prepare for AWS Certification and Advance your Career". That really caught my attention since even though I'd been working with AWS for a little over 2 years, I couldn't call myself an expert because there were so many services and concepts that I'd never heard of.

In order to change that, I started studying and getting my hands dirty with AWS on my own time, and as of today, I hold all three associate certifications available (Developer, Solutions Architect, and SysOps Administrator). One of my goals for 2018 is to keep learning and pass both professional exams (DevOps and Solutions Architect).

Some other topics that also interest me are Python and Big Data, especially the likes of Hadoop and Spark (altough for these technologies, I haven't had the chance to use them professionally, only personally).
